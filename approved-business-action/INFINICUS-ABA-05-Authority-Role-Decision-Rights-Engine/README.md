# INFINICUS ABA-05 — Authority, Role and Decision-Rights Engine

Version: 1.0.0

ABA-05 determines which people, roles, departments, legal entities, or delegated authorities are eligible to approve a governed business action.

Public API:

`window.INFINICUS.ABA.authorityDecisionRightsEngine`

## Capabilities

- Role registry
- Authority-scope registry
- Decision-right registry
- Financial approval limits
- Geographic and legal-entity scope
- Department and action-category authority
- Temporary delegation
- Conflict-of-interest checks
- Authority eligibility evaluation
- ABA-06 approval-policy handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
