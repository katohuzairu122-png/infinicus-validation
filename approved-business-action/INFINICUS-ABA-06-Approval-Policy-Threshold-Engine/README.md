# INFINICUS ABA-06 — Approval Policy and Threshold Engine

Version: 1.0.0

ABA-06 determines the approval requirements that apply to a governed business action based on value, risk, reversibility, impact, sensitivity, scope, and business criticality.

Public API:

`window.INFINICUS.ABA.approvalPolicyThresholdEngine`

## Capabilities

- Approval policy registry
- Threshold rule registry
- Financial-value thresholds
- Risk-severity thresholds
- Reversibility requirements
- Customer, workforce, legal, and data-impact requirements
- Geographic and business-criticality requirements
- Required approver roles and counts
- Sequential, parallel, unanimous, majority, and conditional modes
- ABA-07 workflow handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
