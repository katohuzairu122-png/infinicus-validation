# ABA-09 Instructions

- Load ABA-01 through ABA-08 before ABA-09.
- Generate a contract only from verified, non-revoked approval evidence.
- Preserve the approved decision, action instance, conditions, approvers, target, parameters, constraints, dependencies, outcomes, correlation, lineage, and confidence.
- Do not alter approved conditions.
- Do not execute the action.
- Do not define final parameter boundaries; ABA-10 handles boundaries.
- Record checksum, version, issue time, expiry, and revocation state.
