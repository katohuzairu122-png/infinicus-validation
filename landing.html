// FAQ

Common questions.

Is this a real financial model or a toy?
It's a statistically rigorous simulation — not a toy, but also not a DCF or audited forecast. The Monte Carlo engine runs 500 independent scenarios with Gaussian variance on cost rates, acquisition, and churn. It's designed to surface risk and validate assumptions, not to replace an accountant.
Where is my data stored?
Everything is stored locally in your browser's localStorage — no server, no database. Your name, email, business parameters, and simulation results never leave your device. We collect nothing. Future versions with cloud sync will be opt-in.
How accurate are the industry profiles?
The cost ratios, CAC, and churn rates are calibrated from published benchmarks across African and Middle Eastern markets. They are approximations — your actual numbers will differ based on execution, market conditions, and team quality. The engine is most valuable for directional analysis: "is this structure viable at all?"
Can I export the results?
Yes. The Report & Plan tab includes a full PDF export with executive summary, weekly breakdown, risk analysis, customer metrics, and strategic recommendations. Available on the Starter plan and above.
Do I need to know finance or statistics to use it?
No. You input plain business numbers: how much capital you have, what you charge per sale, how much you'll spend on marketing, and how many people are on your team. The engine handles all the modelling. The verdict is written in plain English.
What is the free plan limit?
The free plan gives you unlimited simulations plus 10 free AI analyses. The AI analyses deliver the full INFINICUS Venture Engine assessment with competitive intelligence, execution roadmap, and risk scoring. After 10 AI analyses, upgrade to Starter ($19/month) or above for unlimited AI.
Can I share my simulation results?
Yes. The Verdict tab includes a Share Link button that encodes your simulation parameters into a URL. Anyone with the link can load your exact inputs and re-run the simulation to verify your results.
