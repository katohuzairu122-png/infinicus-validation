# Architecture

1. Receive DT-12 workforce handoff.
2. Register workforce members.
3. Register reusable skills.
4. Register member capabilities.
5. Register period-based workload and availability state.
6. Validate position, member, and skill references.
7. Calculate utilization, productivity, absence, vacancies, and capability coverage.
8. Detect overloaded members and skill gaps.
9. Create workforce snapshot.
10. Prepare DT-14 asset and infrastructure handoff.
