# Architecture

1. Receive DT-11 inventory handoff.
2. Register products and stock locations.
3. Register suppliers and purchase orders.
4. Register period-based inventory state.
5. Validate product, location, supplier, and order references.
6. Calculate availability, stockouts, days on hand, waste, and supplier performance.
7. Identify reorder candidates.
8. Create inventory-supply snapshot.
9. Preserve operations, customer, and financial context.
10. Prepare DT-13 workforce and capability handoff.
