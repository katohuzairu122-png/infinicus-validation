# Architecture

1. Receive DT-10 operations handoff.
2. Register processes and stages.
3. Register resources and capacity.
4. Register work items and queue state.
5. Validate process, stage, and resource references.
6. Calculate throughput, cycle time, utilization, quality, and SLA.
7. Detect queue bottlenecks.
8. Create operations snapshot.
9. Preserve sales, customer, marketing, and financial context.
10. Prepare DT-12 inventory and supply handoff.
