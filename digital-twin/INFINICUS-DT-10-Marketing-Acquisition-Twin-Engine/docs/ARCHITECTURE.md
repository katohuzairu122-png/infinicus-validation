# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Receive DT-09 marketing handoff.
2. Register marketing channels.
3. Register audiences and segment references.
4. Register campaigns and attribution models.
5. Register period-based campaign state.
6. Validate channel, audience, campaign, and segment references.
7. Calculate engagement, conversion, CAC, ROAS, and ROI.
8. Create marketing-acquisition snapshot.
9. Preserve sales, customer, and financial context.
10. Prepare DT-11 operations and process handoff.

## Responsibility Boundary

DT-10 represents marketing and acquisition state.

DT-11 represents operational workflows, process stages, queues, capacity, throughput, and service performance.
