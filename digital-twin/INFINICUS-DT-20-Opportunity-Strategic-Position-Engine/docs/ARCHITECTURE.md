# Architecture

1. Receive DT-19 opportunity handoff.
2. Register strategic capabilities.
3. Register observed or inferred opportunities.
4. Validate capability and state dependencies.
5. Score expected value, readiness, feasibility, strategic fit, effort, and risk.
6. Calculate gross and risk-adjusted opportunity scores.
7. Detect capability gaps.
8. Rank opportunities and aggregate by domain.
9. Create strategic-position snapshot.
10. Prepare DT-21 integrity and confidence handoff.
