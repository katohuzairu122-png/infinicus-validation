# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Receive DT-05 organization handoff.
2. Register organization units.
3. Register reusable role definitions.
4. Create positions within units.
5. Assign occupants to positions.
6. Define reporting lines.
7. Validate reporting references and cycles.
8. Analyze span of control, layers, vacancies, and leadership gaps.
9. Generate organization chart.
10. Prepare DT-07 financial-state handoff.

## Responsibility Boundary

DT-06 models organization structure.

DT-07 represents the financial state of the business and its organizational units.
