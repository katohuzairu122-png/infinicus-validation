# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Receive DT-07 customer handoff.
2. Register customer segments.
3. Register customer profiles.
4. Attach segment and cohort membership.
5. Register period-based demand state.
6. Record retention, churn, frequency, lifetime value, satisfaction, and advocacy.
7. Validate profile and segment references.
8. Analyze aggregate demand and concentration.
9. Create customer-demand snapshot.
10. Prepare DT-09 sales and revenue-flow handoff.

## Responsibility Boundary

DT-08 represents customer and demand state.

DT-09 represents leads, opportunities, orders, conversions, pipeline, and revenue flows.
