# INFINICUS ABA-02 — Decision Package Intake and Validation Engine

Version: 1.0.0

ABA-02 receives governed decision packages from AI Decision Intelligence and validates whether they may enter the Approved Business Action layer.

Public API: `window.INFINICUS.ABA.decisionPackageIntakeEngine`

Capabilities:
- package intake
- contract validation
- decision-state checks
- simulation evidence checks
- risk and constraint checks
- confidence and lineage checks
- expiry and revocation checks
- checksums
- idempotent intake
- quarantine
- ABA-03 handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
