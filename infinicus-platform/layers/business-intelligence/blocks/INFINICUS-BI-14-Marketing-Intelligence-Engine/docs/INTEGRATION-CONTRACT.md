# BI-10 to BI-14 Contract

BI-14 receives:

- intelligence handoff ID,
- calculation run ID,
- warehouse snapshot ID,
- governed marketing metric results,
- campaign, channel, and audience groups,
- targets,
- threshold status,
- correlation ID.

# BI-14 to BI-19 Contract

BI-14 provides:

- marketing analysis ID,
- marketing profile,
- marketing health score,
- campaign ranking,
- channel ranking,
- audience ranking,
- warning and opportunity signals,
- source metric codes,
- correlation ID.
