# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify marketing metrics.
3. Build a marketing-performance profile.
4. Evaluate reach, engagement, leads, and conversion.
5. Evaluate acquisition cost, ROAS, and campaign ROI.
6. Rank campaigns, channels, and audiences.
7. Calculate marketing health score.
8. Generate warning and opportunity signals.
9. Persist analysis evidence.
10. Publish marketing intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-14 interprets marketing performance.

It does not modify campaigns, bids, budgets, or advertising-platform settings.
