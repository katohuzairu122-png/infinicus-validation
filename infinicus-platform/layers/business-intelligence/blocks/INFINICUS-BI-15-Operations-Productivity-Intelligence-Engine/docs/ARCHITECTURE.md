# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify operations metrics.
3. Build an operations-performance profile.
4. Evaluate throughput, cycle time, capacity, and utilization.
5. Evaluate productivity, SLA compliance, defects, rework, and downtime.
6. Rank processes and locations.
7. Identify likely bottleneck indicators.
8. Calculate operations health score.
9. Generate warning and opportunity signals.
10. Publish operations intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-15 interprets operations performance.

It does not change schedules, resource assignments, or production records.
