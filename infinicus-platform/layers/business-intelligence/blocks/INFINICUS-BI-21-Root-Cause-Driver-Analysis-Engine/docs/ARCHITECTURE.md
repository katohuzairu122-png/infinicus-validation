# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Receive prioritized signals from BI-20.
2. Create an investigation case.
3. Register potential driver hypotheses.
4. Attach supporting and counter evidence.
5. Evaluate five-whys chains and confounders.
6. Score evidence strength.
7. Rank primary and secondary drivers.
8. Construct cause-and-effect graph.
9. Track unresolved hypotheses.
10. Publish investigation results to BI-22.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-21 identifies likely drivers.

It does not claim definitive causation without sufficient evidence.
