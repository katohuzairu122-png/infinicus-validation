# BI-10 to BI-12 Contract

BI-12 receives:

- intelligence handoff ID,
- calculation run ID,
- warehouse snapshot ID,
- governed sales metric results,
- dimensional group results,
- targets,
- threshold status,
- correlation ID.

# BI-12 to BI-19 Contract

BI-12 provides:

- sales analysis ID,
- sales profile,
- sales health score,
- product ranking,
- channel ranking,
- warning and opportunity signals,
- source metric codes,
- correlation ID.
