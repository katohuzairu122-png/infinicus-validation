# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify inventory and supply metrics.
3. Build an inventory profile.
4. Evaluate availability, stockouts, turnover, and days on hand.
5. Evaluate excess, obsolete stock, waste, and shrinkage.
6. Evaluate supplier lead time and reliability.
7. Rank products, warehouses, and suppliers.
8. Calculate inventory health score.
9. Generate warning and opportunity signals.
10. Publish inventory intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-16 interprets inventory and supply performance.

It does not change stock balances, reorder quantities, purchase orders, or supplier records.
