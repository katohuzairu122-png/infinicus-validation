# INFINICUS BI-18 — Market and Competitive Intelligence Engine

Version: 1.0.0

BI-18 interprets governed market and competitor metrics to reveal market position, demand movement, pricing position, competitive pressure, share, differentiation, and external opportunity.

## Capabilities

- Market metric classification
- Market-size and growth interpretation
- Market-share analysis
- Competitive pricing analysis
- Demand and category trend analysis
- Competitor performance comparison
- Positioning and differentiation assessment
- Market, segment, and competitor ranking
- Market health scoring
- Competitive warning and opportunity signals
- BI-19 analysis handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
- Demo interface and automated tests

## Dependencies

- BI-01 through BI-10

## Public API

`window.INFINICUS.BI.marketCompetitiveIntelligenceEngine`
