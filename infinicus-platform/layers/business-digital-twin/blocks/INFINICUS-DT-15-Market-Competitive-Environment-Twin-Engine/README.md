# INFINICUS DT-15 — Market and Competitive Environment Twin Engine

Version: 1.0.0

DT-15 represents market size, market segments, competitors, pricing positions, demand trends, external forces, regulation, competitive intensity, differentiation, and strategic market position inside the Business Digital Twin.

## Capabilities

- Market and segment registry
- Competitor registry
- Pricing-position state
- Market share and demand state
- External-force registry
- Regulation and constraint references
- Competitive intensity analysis
- Differentiation and market-position analysis
- Market concentration and growth analysis
- Competitor comparison matrix
- Market environment snapshots
- DT-16 synchronization handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
- Demo interface and automated tests

## Dependencies

- DT-01 through DT-14

## Public API

`window.INFINICUS.DT.marketCompetitiveTwinEngine`
