# Architecture

1. Receive DT-14 market handoff.
2. Register markets and market segments.
3. Register competitors and competitor coverage.
4. Register market, pricing, demand, and competitive state.
5. Register regulatory and external forces.
6. Validate market, segment, competitor, and force references.
7. Calculate market size, growth, share, intensity, differentiation, and external risk.
8. Rank competitors.
9. Create market environment snapshot.
10. Prepare DT-16 synchronization handoff.
