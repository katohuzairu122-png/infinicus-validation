# Architecture

1. Receive DT-13 asset handoff.
2. Register facilities and locations.
3. Register physical, digital, and logical assets.
4. Register period-based condition and availability state.
5. Register asset dependencies and redundancy.
6. Validate facility, assignment, state, and dependency references.
7. Calculate condition, utilization, availability, downtime, and failure state.
8. Detect critical assets and single points of failure.
9. Create asset-infrastructure snapshot.
10. Prepare DT-15 market and competitive environment handoff.
