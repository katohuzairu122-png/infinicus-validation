# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Receive DT-08 sales handoff.
2. Register pipeline stages.
3. Register opportunities and customer references.
4. Validate stage and customer relationships.
5. Register orders and transaction flows.
6. Register revenue streams and channel associations.
7. Calculate total and weighted pipeline.
8. Calculate conversion, recurring revenue, and sales cycle.
9. Create sales-revenue snapshot.
10. Prepare DT-10 marketing and acquisition handoff.

## Responsibility Boundary

DT-09 represents sales and revenue flow.

DT-10 represents campaigns, audiences, attribution, acquisition economics, and marketing state.
