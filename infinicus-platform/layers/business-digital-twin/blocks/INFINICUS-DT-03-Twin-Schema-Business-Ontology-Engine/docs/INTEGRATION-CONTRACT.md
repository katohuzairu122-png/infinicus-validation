# DT-02 to DT-03 Contract

DT-03 receives:

- schema handoff ID,
- business identity,
- twin identity,
- twin hierarchy,
- owner and tenant metadata,
- lifecycle state,
- requested schema version.

# DT-03 to DT-04 Contract

DT-03 provides:

- intake handoff ID,
- business identity,
- twin identity,
- ontology ID,
- ontology version,
- entity types,
- relationship types,
- state models,
- required attributes,
- ontology constraints.
