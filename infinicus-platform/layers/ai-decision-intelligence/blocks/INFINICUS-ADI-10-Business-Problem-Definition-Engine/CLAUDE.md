# Claude Instructions — ADI-10

- Frame the decision problem; do not generate candidate actions or recommendations.
- Preserve title, statement, desired outcome, scope, constraints, success criteria, assumptions, urgency and status.
- Validate linked goals, triggers and evidence inside the same tenant/business/decision boundary.
- Do not confuse symptoms, causes, constraints and desired outcomes.
- Store every change as an immutable version.
- Do not delete governed history; cancel or supersede instead.
- Publish open problems to ADI-04 through the registered provider.
- Maintain legacy import/export compatibility without editing the original HTML.
