# Integration Contract

1. Attach ADI-01 through ADI-09 first.
2. ADI-10 validates linked goals through ADI-08, triggers through ADI-09 and evidence through ADI-07.
3. ADI-10 registers provider `adi10.problem_definition` with ADI-04.
4. Problem framing may be manual or trigger-driven, but references must remain inside the same boundaries.
5. Problems are versioned and cancelled or superseded instead of deleted.
6. ADI-10 defines the problem only; candidate alternatives begin in ADI-13.
7. Legacy import/export leaves the consolidated HTML untouched.
