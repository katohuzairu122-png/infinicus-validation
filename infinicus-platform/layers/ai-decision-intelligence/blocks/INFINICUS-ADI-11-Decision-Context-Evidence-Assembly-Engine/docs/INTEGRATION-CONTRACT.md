# Integration Contract

1. Attach ADI-01 through ADI-10 first.
2. Supply the secured DecisionCase and matching ADI-04 DecisionContextEnvelope.
3. ADI-11 resolves and verifies ADI-07 evidence and referenced ADI-08–10 records.
4. Required source types are explicit policy inputs, not guessed by ADI-11.
5. Only ready assemblies should proceed to ADI-12 unless governance explicitly permits needs-data handling.
6. ADI-11 assembles evidence but makes no business recommendation.
