# Integration Contract

1. Attach ADI-01 through ADI-11 first.
2. Use a ready ADI-11 assembly or record a governance override.
3. Objectives, constraints, criteria and weights must come from authorized stakeholders or policy.
4. Numeric constraints and quantitative criteria require explicit units.
5. Validate and lock the framework before ADI-13 generates alternatives.
6. ADI-12 defines evaluation rules but performs no option scoring or recommendation.
