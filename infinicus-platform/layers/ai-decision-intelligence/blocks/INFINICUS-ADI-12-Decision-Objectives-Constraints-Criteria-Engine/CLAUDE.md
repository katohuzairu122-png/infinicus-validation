# Claude Instructions — ADI-12

- Keep business goals separate from decision-specific objectives.
- Require a ready ADI-11 assembly unless an explicit governance override is recorded.
- Never invent constraints, criteria, thresholds or weights.
- Distinguish hard constraints from soft preferences.
- Preserve metric units, operators, target values and evidence references.
- Normalize supplied criterion weights transparently; reject zero-total or negative weights.
- Lock validated frameworks before downstream alternative scoring.
- Do not generate alternatives, simulate scenarios, score options or recommend actions.
