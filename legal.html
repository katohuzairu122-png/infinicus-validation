AI Disclaimer
⚠ CRITICAL DISCLAIMER — READ BEFORE ACTING ON ANY RESULT

The GO, MODIFY, and STOP verdicts produced by INFINICUS ENGINE are outputs of a statistical simulation model. They are NOT guarantees, predictions, or professional advice of any kind. Do not invest real money, quit your job, or make major life decisions based solely on a simulation result.

What the Engine Actually Does

INFINICUS ENGINE runs a stochastic 60-day simulation using industry-average parameters, Monte Carlo sampling (500 runs), and user-supplied inputs. It models probable cash flow trajectories under randomised conditions. The AI analysis is generated by Anthropic's Claude model based on the simulation outputs.

What It Cannot Do

  • It cannot predict actual market conditions, competitor actions, regulatory changes, or macroeconomic events.
  • It cannot account for your personal execution ability, relationships, or domain expertise.
  • It cannot verify whether your input assumptions (price, demand, costs) are accurate for your specific market.
  • It cannot replace a business plan, financial model, market research, or professional due diligence.
  • It cannot provide legal, tax, accounting, or investment advice.

AI-Generated Content

The AI analysis (headline, summary, actions, investor note) is generated by a large language model (Anthropic Claude). AI outputs may contain errors, hallucinations, or outdated information. Always verify AI-generated recommendations with domain experts.

No Warranty

The Service is provided "as is" without any warranty of accuracy, completeness, or fitness for purpose. Simulation results will differ from real-world outcomes.
