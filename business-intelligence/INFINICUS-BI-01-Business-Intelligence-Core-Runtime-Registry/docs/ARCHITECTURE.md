# Architecture

BI-01 establishes the runtime contract shared by the entire Business Intelligence layer.

## Core Registries

- services
- routes
- datasets
- metrics
- connectors

## Runtime Responsibilities

1. Create structured result envelopes.
2. Register Business Intelligence services.
3. Register and invoke layer routes.
4. Publish and subscribe to events.
5. Register datasets and metrics.
6. Report layer diagnostics.
7. Expose the 24-block Business Intelligence manifest.
