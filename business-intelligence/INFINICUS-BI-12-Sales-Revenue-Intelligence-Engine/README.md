# INFINICUS BI-12 — Sales and Revenue Intelligence Engine

Version: 1.0.0

BI-12 interprets sales and revenue metrics to reveal growth, conversion, customer value, product performance, channel performance, pipeline strength, and revenue concentration.

## Capabilities

- Sales metric classification
- Revenue growth analysis
- Product and service performance
- Channel performance analysis
- Sales conversion analysis
- Average order value intelligence
- Pipeline and win-rate analysis
- Revenue concentration analysis
- Sales health scoring
- Sales opportunity and warning signals
- BI-19 analysis handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
- Demo interface and automated tests

## Dependencies

- BI-01 through BI-10

## Public API

`window.INFINICUS.BI.salesRevenueIntelligenceEngine`
