# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify sales and revenue metrics.
3. Build a sales-performance profile.
4. Evaluate growth, conversion, win rate, order value, and pipeline.
5. Rank product and channel groups.
6. Evaluate revenue concentration.
7. Calculate sales health score.
8. Generate warning and opportunity signals.
9. Persist analysis evidence.
10. Publish sales intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-12 interprets sales performance.

It does not change CRM records, pricing, orders, or sales assignments.
