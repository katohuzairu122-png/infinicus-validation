# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify workforce metrics.
3. Build workforce profile.
4. Evaluate staffing, attendance, retention, and turnover.
5. Evaluate productivity, workload, engagement, and skills.
6. Rank teams, roles, and locations.
7. Calculate workforce health score.
8. Generate workforce warning and opportunity signals.
9. Persist analysis evidence.
10. Publish workforce intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-17 interprets workforce and organizational performance.

It does not alter HR, payroll, scheduling, or performance-management records.
