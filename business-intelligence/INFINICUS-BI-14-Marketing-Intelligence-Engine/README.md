# INFINICUS BI-14 — Marketing Intelligence Engine

Version: 1.0.0

BI-14 interprets marketing performance metrics to reveal reach, engagement, conversion, acquisition efficiency, campaign return, channel contribution, and audience performance.

## Capabilities

- Marketing metric classification
- Reach and engagement analysis
- Lead and conversion intelligence
- Customer acquisition cost analysis
- Return on ad spend analysis
- Campaign ROI analysis
- Channel attribution interpretation
- Audience and campaign ranking
- Marketing health scoring
- Marketing warning and opportunity signals
- BI-19 analysis handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
- Demo interface and automated tests

## Dependencies

- BI-01 through BI-10

## Public API

`window.INFINICUS.BI.marketingIntelligenceEngine`
