# BI-10 to BI-13 Contract

BI-13 receives:

- intelligence handoff ID,
- calculation run ID,
- warehouse snapshot ID,
- governed customer metric results,
- cohort and segment groups,
- targets,
- threshold status,
- correlation ID.

# BI-13 to BI-19 Contract

BI-13 provides:

- customer analysis ID,
- customer profile,
- customer health score,
- cohort ranking,
- warning and opportunity signals,
- source metric codes,
- correlation ID.
