# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify customer metrics.
3. Build customer profile.
4. Evaluate acquisition, activation, retention, churn, and engagement.
5. Evaluate customer lifetime value and satisfaction.
6. Rank customer cohorts and segments.
7. Calculate customer health score.
8. Generate warning and opportunity signals.
9. Persist analysis evidence.
10. Publish customer intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-13 interprets customer performance.

It does not change customer, CRM, or campaign records.
