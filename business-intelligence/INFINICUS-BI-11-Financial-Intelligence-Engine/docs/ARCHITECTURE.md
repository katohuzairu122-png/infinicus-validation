# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify financial metrics.
3. Build a financial profile.
4. Evaluate profitability and margins.
5. Evaluate cash flow, liquidity, burn, and runway.
6. Evaluate budget variance.
7. Calculate financial health score.
8. Generate financial risk signals.
9. Persist analysis evidence.
10. Publish financial intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-11 interprets financial metrics.

It does not execute accounting entries, payments, or treasury actions.
