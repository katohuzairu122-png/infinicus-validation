# Architecture

## Processing Sequence

1. Load BI-10 intelligence handoff.
2. Identify market and competitive metrics.
3. Build market profile.
4. Evaluate market size, growth, demand, and share.
5. Evaluate pricing position, competitive intensity, and differentiation.
6. Rank markets, segments, and competitors.
7. Calculate market health score.
8. Generate competitive warning and opportunity signals.
9. Persist analysis evidence.
10. Publish market intelligence to BI-19.

## Responsibility Boundary

BI-18 interprets market and competitor performance.

It does not create unsupported competitor facts or execute market actions.
