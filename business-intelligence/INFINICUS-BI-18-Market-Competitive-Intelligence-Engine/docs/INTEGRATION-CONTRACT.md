# BI-10 to BI-18 Contract

BI-18 receives:

- intelligence handoff ID,
- calculation run ID,
- warehouse snapshot ID,
- governed market and competitor metric results,
- market, segment, and competitor groups,
- targets,
- threshold status,
- correlation ID.

# BI-18 to BI-19 Contract

BI-18 provides:

- market analysis ID,
- market profile,
- market health score,
- market ranking,
- segment ranking,
- competitor ranking,
- warning and opportunity signals,
- correlation ID.
