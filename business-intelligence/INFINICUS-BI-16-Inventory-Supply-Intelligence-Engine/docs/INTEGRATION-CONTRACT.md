# BI-10 to BI-16 Contract

BI-16 receives:

- intelligence handoff ID,
- calculation run ID,
- warehouse snapshot ID,
- governed inventory and supply metric results,
- product, warehouse, and supplier groups,
- targets,
- threshold status,
- correlation ID.

# BI-16 to BI-19 Contract

BI-16 provides:

- inventory analysis ID,
- inventory profile,
- inventory health score,
- product-risk ranking,
- warehouse ranking,
- supplier ranking,
- warning and opportunity signals,
- correlation ID.
