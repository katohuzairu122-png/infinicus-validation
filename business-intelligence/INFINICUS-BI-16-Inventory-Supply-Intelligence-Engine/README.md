# INFINICUS BI-16 — Inventory and Supply Intelligence Engine

Version: 1.0.0

BI-16 interprets inventory and supply metrics to reveal stock health, turnover, availability, replenishment, supplier reliability, lead time, waste, and supply risk.

## Capabilities

- Inventory and supply metric classification
- Stock availability and stockout analysis
- Inventory turnover and days-on-hand analysis
- Reorder and replenishment intelligence
- Excess and obsolete stock detection
- Waste and shrinkage analysis
- Supplier lead-time and reliability analysis
- Product, warehouse, and supplier ranking
- Inventory health scoring
- Inventory warning and opportunity signals
- BI-19 analysis handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
- Demo interface and automated tests

## Dependencies

- BI-01 through BI-10

## Public API

`window.INFINICUS.BI.inventorySupplyIntelligenceEngine`
