# INFINICUS BI-24 — Business Digital Twin Publication and Handoff Engine

Version: 1.0.0

BI-24 packages governed Business Intelligence outputs into a Business Digital Twin state publication.

Public API:

`window.INFINICUS.BI.digitalTwinPublicationEngine`

## Capabilities

- Publication policy registry
- Digital Twin destination registry
- Business-state package generation
- Domain intelligence aggregation
- Metric and KPI snapshot publication
- Risk and anomaly publication
- Data-quality and confidence preservation
- Intelligence lineage preservation
- Idempotent publication
- Delivery receipts and dead letters
- BI-25 integration handoff
- IndexedDB persistence
